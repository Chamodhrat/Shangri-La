About Us


Nestled along the picturesque Galle Face Green promenade, Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo, stands as an iconic beacon of hospitality, elegance, and luxury in the vibrant city of Colombo, Sri Lanka. With a rich heritage of world-class service and a commitment to exceeding guest expectations, Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo, is a sanctuary of indulgence for both business and leisure travelers.



History


Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo, opened its doors to guests in 1980, marking a significant milestone in Sri Lanka's hospitality industry. As part of the renowned Shangri-La Hotels and Resorts group, it continues the tradition of excellence and impeccable service that the brand is known for worldwide. The hotel's location on Galle Face Green holds historical significance, as it was once a colonial-era gathering place and remains an iconic landmark in Colombo. Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo, pays homage to this history while embracing the city's vibrant present and future. With its blend of modern luxury and a nod to Colombo's heritage, Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo, stands as a testament to the city's evolution as a cosmopolitan hub, where guests can experience a harmonious fusion of comfort, culture, and sophisticatio
