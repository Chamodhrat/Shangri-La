Unleash Your Inner Explorer: Shangri-La Expedition - Discover the Uncharted


Adventure overview


Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime adventure with Shangri-La as we invite you to "Discover the Uncharted." Leave the ordinary behind and embrace the thrill of the unknown as we take you on an epic expedition that combines luxury with untamed exploration.


Remote Wilderness Retreat


Begin your journey at Hotel Shangri-La, Colombo, where you'll be introduced to the expedition team and your fellow adventurers. After a warm welcome dinner, prepare to leave civilization behind.


Luxurious Camping


Experience the wilderness like never before with upscale camping in exotic locations. Our expert guides will lead you to remote, untouched landscapes, where you'll sleep in luxurious tents equipped with all the comforts you need.


Starry Nights:


Experience the magic of starry nights in the wilderness. Our campfires, stargazing sessions, and storytelling under the open sky will leave you with unforgettable memories.
